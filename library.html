Library

This is a collection of books that I've read lately. I'll do my best to make some notes about each title, although they'll remain far from a formal review and will try more to capture my impression of the work. I've not yet practiced sitting and reflecting after finishing each read, so I imagine neither my approach nor observations will be particularly sophisticated. I hope that through the practice of keeping records here, I will improve at reflecting on the books I read.
